# Water Safety (Recreational)

<p>Playing in the water - whether swimming, boating or diving - can be fun. It can also be dangerous, especially for children. Being safe can help prevent injuries and <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/drowning.html">drowning</a>. To stay safe in the water:</p><ul>
<li>Avoid alcohol when swimming or boating</li>
<li>Wear a life jacket whenever you're in a boat</li>
<li>Don't swim alone, or in bad weather</li>
<li>Learn <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/cpr.html">CPR</a></li>
<li>Learn to swim and teach your children to swim</li>
<li>Supervise your children when they are in the water</li>
<li>Prevent sunburns - use plenty of sunscreen</li>
</ul>
