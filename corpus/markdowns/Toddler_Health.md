# Toddler Health

<p>Most young children get sick. It is hard for parents to know what is serious. You can learn what the common warning signs are. In the end, trust your intuition. If you are worried about your toddler, call your health care provider right away.</p>

<p>Well-child visits are important to your toddler's health. Toddlers will get their recommended <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/childhoodvaccines.html">vaccines</a> during these visits. Routine exams and screenings help you and your kids prevent and treat health problems as well as chart their <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/toddlerdevelopment.html">growth and development</a>.</p>
