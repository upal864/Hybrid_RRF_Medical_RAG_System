# Compulsive Gambling

<p>Many people enjoy gambling, whether it's betting on a horse or playing poker on the Internet. Most people who gamble don't have a problem, but some lose control of their gambling. Signs of problem gambling include :</p><ul>
<li>	Always thinking about gambling</li>
<li>	Lying about gambling</li>
<li>	Spending work or family time gambling </li>
<li>	Feeling bad after you gamble, but not quitting  </li>
<li>	Gambling with money you need for other things</li>	
</ul>

<p>If you have concerns about your gambling, ask for help. Your health care provider can work with you to find the treatment that's best for you.</p>
