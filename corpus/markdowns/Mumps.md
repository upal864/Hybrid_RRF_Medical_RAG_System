# Mumps

<p>Mumps is an illness caused by the mumps virus. It starts with:</p><ul>
    <li>Fever</li>
  <li>Headache</li>
  <li>Muscle aches</li>
  <li>Tiredness</li>
  <li>Loss of appetite</li>
</ul>

<p>After that, the salivary glands under the ears or jaw become swollen and tender. The swelling can be on one or both sides of the face.  Symptoms last 7 to 10 days. Serious complications are rare.</p>

<p>You can catch mumps by being with another person who has it.  There is no treatment for mumps, but the measles-mumps-rubella (MMR) vaccine can prevent it.</p>

<p>Before the routine vaccination program in the United States, mumps was a common illness in infants, children and young adults. Now it is a rare disease in the U.S.</p>
