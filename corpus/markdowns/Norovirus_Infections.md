# Norovirus Infections

<p>Noroviruses are a group of related viruses. Infection with these viruses causes an illness called <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/gastroenteritis.html">gastroenteritis</a>, an inflammation of the stomach and intestines.  It can spread from person to person, or through contaminated food or water.  You can also get it if you touch a contaminated surface. Norovirus can be serious, especially for young children and older adults.</p>

<p>The most common symptoms of norovirus infection are:</p><ul>
<li>Diarrhea</li>
<li>Nausea and vomiting</li>
<li>Stomach pain</li>
</ul>

<p>Other symptoms may include fever, headache or body aches.</p>

<p>Treatment includes bed rest and lots of liquids to prevent <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/dehydration.html">dehydration</a>. There is no specific medicine to treat norovirus infections.</p>

<p>Proper hand washing and safe food preparation may help prevent infections.</p>
