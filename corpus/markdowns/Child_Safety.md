# Child Safety

<p>As parents, we want to keep our children safe from harm. Take steps to keep your children safe:</p>
<ul>
<li>Install the right child safety seat in your <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/motorvehiclesafety.html">car</a></li>
<li>Teach children how to cross the street safely</li>
<li>Make sure they wear the right gear and equipment for <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/sportssafety.html">sports</a></li>
<li>Install and test smoke alarms </li>
<li>Store medicines, cleaners and other <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/poisoning.html">dangerous substances</a> in locked cabinets</li>
<li>Babyproof your home</li>
<li>Don't leave small children unattended</li>
</ul>
