# Preterm Labor

<p>Preterm labor is labor that starts before 37 completed weeks of pregnancy. It can lead to premature birth. <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/prematurebabies.html">Premature babies</a> may face serious health risks. </p>

<p>Symptoms of preterm labor include:</p><ul>
<li>Contractions every 10 minutes or more often</li>
<li>Leaking fluid or bleeding from the vagina</li>
<li>Feeling of pressure in the pelvis</li>
<li>Low, dull backache</li>
<li>Cramps that feel like menstrual cramps</li>
<li>Abdominal cramps with or without diarrhea</li>
</ul>

<p> If you think you might be having preterm labor, contact your health care provider. </p>
