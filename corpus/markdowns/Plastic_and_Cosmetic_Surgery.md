# Plastic and Cosmetic Surgery

<p>Surgeons can reshape the appearance of body parts through cosmetic surgery.  Some of the most common body parts people want to improve through surgery include:</p><ul>
<li> Breasts: Increase or reduce the size of breasts or reshape sagging breasts </li> 
<li> 	Ears: Reduce the size of large ears or set protruding ears back closer to the head</li> 
<li> 	Eyes: Correct drooping upper eyelids or remove puffy bags below the eyes</li> 
<li> 	Face: Remove facial wrinkles, creases or acne scars</li> 
<li> 	Hair: Fill in balding areas with one's own hair</li> 
<li> 	Nose: Change the shape of the nose </li> 
<li> 	Tummy:  Flatten the abdomen</li> 
</ul>
