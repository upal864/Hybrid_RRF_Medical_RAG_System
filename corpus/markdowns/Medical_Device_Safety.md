# Medical Device Safety

<p>A medical device is any product used to diagnose, cure, or treat a condition, or to prevent disease. They range from small and simple, like a blood glucose meter, to large and complicated, like a ventilator. You might use one at home or at work, or you may need one in a hospital.</p>

<p>To use medical devices safely:</p><ul>
<li>Know how your device works. Keep instructions close by</li>
<li>Understand and properly respond to device alarms</li>
<li>Have a back-up plan and supplies in the event of an emergency</li>
<li>Keep emergency numbers available and update them as needed</li>
<li>Educate your family and caregivers about your device</li>
</ul>
