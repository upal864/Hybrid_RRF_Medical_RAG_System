# Choosing a Doctor or Health Care Service

<p>We all want high-quality health care, but it's hard to know how to choose. There are many things to consider, including:</p><ul>
<li>What your insurance covers</li>
<li>Whether a health care provider or service is accredited</li>
<li>The location of a service</li>
<li>Hours that the service is available</li>
<li>Whether you like a health care provider's personality </li>
</ul>

<p>On this page you'll find information to help you choose a health care provider or service.</p>
