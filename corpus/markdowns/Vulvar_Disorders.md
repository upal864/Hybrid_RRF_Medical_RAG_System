# Vulvar Disorders

<p>The vulva is the external part of a woman's genitals. Some problems you can have with the vulvar area include:</p><ul>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vaginitis.html">Vaginitis</a> or vulvovaginitis, swelling or infection of the vulva and vagina</li>	
<li>Skin problems due to allergy </li>	
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vulvarcancer.html">Vulvar cancer</a></li>	
<li>Vulvodynia, or vulvar pain</li>	
</ul>

<p>Symptoms may include redness, itching, pain, or cracks in the skin. Treatment depends on the cause.</p>
