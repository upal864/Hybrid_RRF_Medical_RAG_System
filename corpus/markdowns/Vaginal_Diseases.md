# Vaginal Diseases

<p>Vaginal problems are some of the most common reasons women go to the doctor. They may have symptoms such as:</p><ul>
<li>Itching</li>
<li>Burning</li>
<li>Pain</li>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vaginalbleeding.html">Abnormal bleeding</a></li>
<li>Discharge</li>
</ul>

<p>One common problem is <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vaginitis.html">vaginitis</a>, an inflammation of the vagina.  Other problems that affect the vagina include <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/sexuallytransmittedinfections.html">sexually transmitted infections</a> (STIs), <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vaginalcancer.html">vaginal cancer</a>, and <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vulvarcancer.html">vulvar cancer</a>. Treatment of vaginal problems depends on the cause.</p>
