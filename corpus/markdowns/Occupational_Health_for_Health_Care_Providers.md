# Occupational Health for Health Care Providers

<p>Health care workers are exposed to many job hazards. These can include :</p><ul>
<li>	Infections</li>
<li>Needle injuries</li>
<li>	Back injuries</li>
<li>	Allergy-causing substances</li>
<li>	Violence</li>
<li>	Stress</li>
</ul>
<p>Follow good job safety and injury prevention practices. They can reduce your risk of health problems. Use protective equipment, follow <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/infectioncontrol.html">infection control</a> guidelines, learn the right way to lift heavy objects, and find ways to manage stress. </p>
