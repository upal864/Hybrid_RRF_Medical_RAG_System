# Men's Health

<p>Most men need to pay more attention to their health. Compared to women, men are more likely to:</p><ul>
<li>Smoke and drink</li>
<li>Make unhealthy or risky choices</li>
<li>Put off regular checkups and medical care</li>
</ul>

<p>There are also health conditions that only affect men, such as <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/prostatecancer.html">prostate cancer</a> and low testosterone. Many of the major health risks that men face - like <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/colorectalcancer.html">colon cancer</a> or <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/heartdiseases.html">heart disease</a> - can be prevented and treated with early diagnosis. Screening tests can find diseases early, when they are easier to treat. It's important to get the screening tests you need.</p>
