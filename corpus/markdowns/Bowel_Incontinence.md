# Bowel Incontinence

<p>Bowel incontinence is the inability to control your bowels. When you feel the urge to have a <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/bowelmovement.html">bowel movement</a>, you may not be able to hold it until you get to a toilet. Millions of Americans have this problem. It affects people of all ages - children and adults. It is more common in women and older adults. It is not a normal part of aging.</p>

<p>Causes include:</p><ul>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/constipation.html">Constipation</a></li>
<li>Damage to muscles or nerves of the anus and rectum</li>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/diarrhea.html">Diarrhea</a></li>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/pelvicfloordisorders.html">Pelvic floor disorders</a></li>
</ul>

<p>Treatments include changes in diet, medicines, bowel training, or surgery. </p>
