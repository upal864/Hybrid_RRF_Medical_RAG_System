# School Health

<p>Your child spends more time at school than anywhere else except home. Schools can have a major effect on children's health. Schools can teach children about health, and promote healthy behaviors. Physical education classes give children a chance to get exercise.</p>

<p>Schools work to:</p><ul>
<li>Prevent risky behaviors such as <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/underagedrinking.html">alcohol</a> and <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/smokingandyouth.html">tobacco</a> use or <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/bullyingandcyberbullying.html">bullying and cyberbullying</a></li>
<li>Encourage healthy habits like exercise and healthy eating</li> 
<li>Deal with specific health problems in students, such as <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/asthmainchildren.html">asthma</a>, <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/obesityinchildren.html">obesity</a> and infectious diseases</li>
</ul>

<p>The school building and environment should be a safe and healthy place for your child.</p>
