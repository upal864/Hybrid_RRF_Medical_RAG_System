# Vitamin C

<p><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/vitamins.html">Vitamins</a> are substances that your body needs to grow and develop normally. Vitamin C is an <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/antioxidants.html">antioxidant</a>. It is important for your skin, bones, and connective tissue.  It promotes healing and helps the body absorb iron.</p>

<p>Vitamin C comes from fruits and vegetables.  Good sources include citrus, red and green peppers, tomatoes, broccoli, and greens. Some juices and cereals have added vitamin C.</p>

<p>Some people may need extra vitamin C:</p>
<ul>
<li>Pregnant/breastfeeding women</li>
<li>Smokers</li>
<li>People recovering from surgery</li>
<li>Burn victims</li>
</ul>
