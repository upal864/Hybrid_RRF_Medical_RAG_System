# Secondhand Smoke

<p>Secondhand smoke is a mixture of the smoke that comes from the burning end of a cigarette, cigar, or pipe, and the smoke breathed out by the smoker.  It contains more than 7,000 chemicals. Hundreds of those chemicals are toxic and about 70 can cause cancer.</p>

<p>Health effects of secondhand smoke include:</p><ul>
<li>Ear infections in children</li>
<li>More frequent and severe asthma attacks in children</li>
<li>Heart disease and lung cancer in adults who have never smoked</li>
</ul>

<p>There is no safe amount of secondhand smoke. Even low levels of it can be harmful. The only way to fully protect nonsmokers from secondhand smoke is not to allow <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/smoking.html">smoking</a> indoors.</p>
