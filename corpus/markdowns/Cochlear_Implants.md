# Cochlear Implants

<p>A cochlear implant is a small, complex electronic device that can help to provide a sense of sound. People who are profoundly <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/hearingdisordersanddeafness.html">deaf</a> or severely hard-of-hearing can get help from them. The implant consists of two parts. One part sits on the outside of the body, behind the ear.  A second part is surgically placed under the skin. 
</p>
<p>
An implant does not restore normal hearing.  It can help a person understand speech.  Children and adults can benefit from them.
</p>
