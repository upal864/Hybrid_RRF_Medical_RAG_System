# Health Disparities

<p>Health disparities are health differences between different groups of people. These health differences may include:</p><ul>
<li>How many people get certain diseases</li>
<li>How severe the diseases are</li>
<li>How many people have complications because of the diseases</li>
<li>How many people die from a disease</li>
<li>Whether people can get health care</li>
<li>How many people get screened for a disease</li>
</ul>

<p>These groups of people may be based on:</p><ul>
<li>Race</li>
<li>Ethnicity</li>
<li>Immigrant status</li>
<li>Disability</li>
<li>Sex or gender</li>
<li>Sexual orientation</li>
<li>Geography</li>
<li>Income</li>
<li>Level of education</li>
</ul>
