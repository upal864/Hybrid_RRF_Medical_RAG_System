# Sexual Health

<p>Sexuality is a big part of being human. Love, affection and sexual intimacy all play a role in healthy relationships. They also contribute to your sense of well-being. A number of disorders can affect the ability to have or enjoy sex  in both <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/sexualproblemsinmen.html">men</a> and <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/sexualproblemsinwomen.html">women</a>. </p>

<p>Factors that can affect sexual health include:</p><ul>
<li>Fear of unplanned pregnancy</li>
<li>Concerns about <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/infertility.html">infertility</a></li>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/sexuallytransmittedinfections.html">Sexually transmitted infections</a> (STIs)</li>
<li>Chronic diseases such as cancer or heart disease</li>
<li>Medicines that affect sexual desire or performance</li>
</ul>
