# Motor Vehicle Safety

<p>Every year thousands of people in the U.S. die from motor vehicle crashes. Trying to prevent these crashes is one part of motor vehicle safety.  Here are some things you can do to be safer on the road:</p>

<ul>
<li> Make sure your vehicle is safe and in working order</li>
<li> Use car seats for children</li> 
<li> Wear your seat belt</li> 
<li> Don't speed or drive aggressively</li>
<li> Don't drive <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/impaireddriving.html">impaired</a></li>
</ul>

<p>Safety also involves being aware of others. Share the road with bicycles and motorcycles, and watch for pedestrians.</p>
