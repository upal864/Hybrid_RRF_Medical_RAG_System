# Sexual Problems in Men

<p>Many men have sexual problems. They become more common as men age.  Problems can include:</p><ul>
<li><a href="https://medlineplus.gov/erectiledysfunction.html">Erectile dysfunction</a></li>
<li>Reduced or lost interest in sex</li>
<li>Problems with ejaculation</li>
<li>Low testosterone</li>
</ul>

<p>Stress, illness, medicines, or emotional problems may also be factors.  Occasional problems with sexual function are common. If problems last more than a few months or cause distress for you or your partner, you should see your health care provider.</p>
