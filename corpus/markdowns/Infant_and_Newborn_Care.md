# Infant and Newborn Care

<p>Going home with a new baby is exciting, but it can be scary, too. Newborns have many needs, like frequent feedings and diaper changes. Babies can have <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/commoninfantandnewbornproblems.html">health issues</a> that are different from older children and adults, like diaper rash and cradle cap. </p>

<p>Your baby will go through many <a href="https://medlineplus.gov/infantandnewborndevelopment.html">changes</a> during the first year of life. You may feel uneasy at first. Ask your health care provider for help if you need it.</p>
